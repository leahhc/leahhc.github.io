Let's Get Into It Shall We?

Step 1: Gather Your Ingredients

  • Eggs (as many as you’d like, but I recommend starting with 6)
  • Water
  • Ice (for the ice bath)

Step 2: Prep The Pot

  • Place your eggs gently in a pot, giving them enough room to breathe. Don’t overcrowd them!
  • Fill the pot with cold water until the eggs are covered by about 1 inch.
